Everything you need to know about Klyd Analytics

How quickly can I see results with Klyd Analytics?

Most customers see improved performance within the first week of implementation. Our AI algorithms start optimizing your campaigns immediately, and you'll typically see significant ROI improvements within 30 days.

What platforms does Klyd integrate with?

Klyd integrates with all major advertising platforms including Google Ads, Facebook Ads, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads, Twitter Ads, and many more. We also support direct API integrations with your existing marketing tools.

Is there a setup fee or long-term contract?

No setup fees and no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime. We offer a 14-day free trial so you can test our platform risk-free before committing to a paid plan.

How secure is my advertising data?

We use enterprise-grade security with 256-bit SSL encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and regular security audits. Your data is never shared with third parties and is stored in secure, encrypted databases.

Yes! We offer custom enterprise plans with dedicated account managers, custom integrations, advanced reporting, and priority support. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.
